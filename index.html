League of Humanist Makers

We are are the Humanist Makers and we are the dreamers of dreams.

We believe in rough consensus and running code.

We believe objects embed ideas and ideas embed objects.

We seek knowledge through a theory of praxis and a praxis of theory.

We believe that Making is a state of mind, expressed in code, in materials, through sound and motion and fury.

We believe Perfect is the enemy of the Good is the enemy of Done.

We believe in the humanist sentiment,

And we believe all humans are makers.
